About Devil App Store

Our Mission

At Devil App Store, our mission is to empower independent developers and creators across India. We believe that everyone with a great idea deserves a platform to showcase their talent without facing high entry barriers. Our goal is to provide a free, open, and secure marketplace where developers can publish their Android apps, ebooks, and music, and connect with a wide audience.

Our Story

Devil App Store was founded in 2025 by Devil Group with a simple vision: to create an alternative to mainstream app stores that often come with complex policies and revenue-sharing models. We wanted to build a community-driven platform where innovation and creativity are the main focus. We are passionate about technology and supporting the incredible talent that exists in the developer community.

Why Choose Us?

Free to Publish: We don't charge any fees for publishing your apps. You can upload and manage your creations for free.

Monetization Ready: We provide the foundation for you to monetize your apps in the future, whether through ads or by setting a price for your content.

Security and Trust: Our Aadhaar-based developer verification system ensures a trusted environment for both developers and users, reducing the risk of malicious software.
