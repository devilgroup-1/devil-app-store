Refund Policy

Last Updated: 02 September, 2025

General Policy

Thank you for your purchases at Devil App Store. We value our customers and strive to provide high-quality digital products.

Digital Products

Due to the digital nature of our products (including apps, ebooks, and music), all sales are final once the download has been initiated. We generally do not offer refunds or exchanges for any digital products purchased through our platform. We encourage you to review the app details, screenshots, and descriptions carefully before making a purchase to ensure it meets your needs.

Exceptional Circumstances for Refunds

We may consider issuing a refund at our sole discretion under the following exceptional circumstances:

  • Technical Issues: If the product is proven to be technically defective and our support team is unable to resolve the issue for you.
  • Incorrect Product: If you were delivered a product different from what you purchased.
  • Duplicate Purchase: If a technical error resulted in you being charged multiple times for the same single product.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund based on the exceptional circumstances listed above, you must contact our support team within 7 days of your purchase.

Please email us at devilgroup1@gmail.com with the following information:

  • Your full name and the email address used for the purchase.
  • The name of the product you purchased.
  • Your order number or transaction ID.
  • A clear explanation of why you are requesting a refund.

Each request will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Devil App Store reserves the right to approve or deny any refund request.
